title: Beyond Dark Mode
date: 05 May 2026
tag: Visual Language

---

Dark mode is not just a color swap. Many products place white text on a black background and call the job finished, without adapting the rest of the interface.

A thoughtful dark theme requires a fresh look at light, shadow, and contrast. Our eyes read the same colors differently as the surrounding light changes, so values that work in a bright theme may feel harsh in a dark one.

Dark mode is also an accessibility concern. For people who are sensitive to bright light, it can make the difference between a comfortable product and one they cannot use for long.

When building a palette, visual style is only one part of the work. Contrast, readability, and accessibility matter just as much. Good design becomes beautiful through function.
